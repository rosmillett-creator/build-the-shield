How DysPhilter™ Can Support You
Four things it does — simply

Protects you from harmful messages

If someone sends you something hostile, manipulative, or degrading — DysPhilter™ catches it before it reaches you. You do not have to read it. The evidence is kept safely.

Carries your needs into every conversation

You should not have to explain yourself over and over. DysPhilter™ holds your access needs and carries them into every interaction, meeting, and appointment.

Keeps your evidence safe

Every harmful message caught is timestamped and sealed. It can go to your lawyer, your union, or HR — without you ever having to re-read it.

Helps when you are overwhelmed

When everything is too much, DysPhilter™ gives you space to find your feet. No pressure. No checklist. Just a quiet place to settle.

How to Get It Funded
Access to Work — a grant you do not pay back
1

Check you are eligible

If you have a disability, health condition, or neurodivergent profile that affects your work — you can apply. Employed, self-employed, or about to start work. No formal diagnosis required in all cases.

2

Apply

Apply at gov.uk/access-to-work or call 0800 121 7479. Name DysPhilter™ as your preferred tool.

3

Workplace needs assessment

An adviser will arrange a workplace needs assessment. You can request DysPhilter™ as part of your recommendations.

4

Get your recommendations

Your AtW report will list DysPhilter™ and any other tools or coaching recommended for you.

5

Order through your supplier

Send your AtW report to your assistive technology supplier. They supply DysPhilter™ and provide training.

6

Reimbursement

Your employer purchases the licence and claims back from DWP. Self-employed? You claim directly.

Self-employed? You are eligible for 100% funding. No employer needed. Call 0800 121 7479.

The grant does not affect your other benefits. You do not pay it back.
