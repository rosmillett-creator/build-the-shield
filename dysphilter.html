1

The Workplace Barrier Solved

Actively mitigates severe executive dysfunction, sensory overwhelm, cognitive fatigue, Rejection Sensitive Dysphoria, processing differences (dyslexia, dyspraxia, dysgraphia, dyscalculia, SPLD), colour vision differences, hearing differences, Tourette's syndrome, and communication-triggered distress by providing a send-side intercept that filters hostile or harmful content before it reaches the neurodivergent user — combined with a Universal Passport that carries their access needs, communication preferences, and evidence into every interaction without requiring them to explain themselves repeatedly.

2

How It Works (SaaS / Middleware Integration)

Operates as a seamless digital accessibility layer that sits quietly between standard corporate communication tools (email, messaging platforms, institutional portals) and the end-user. It transforms overwhelming communication into plain-language summaries, filters harmful content before delivery, provides regulation tools matched to the user's current capacity, and creates a predictable, customisable, focus-friendly experience — without storing or reading the content it processes.

Access to Work — How to Get DysPhilter™ Funded
A government grant that pays for assistive software
1

Check you are eligible

You can apply if you have a disability, health condition, or neurodivergent profile that affects your ability to work. You must be employed, self-employed, or about to start work. No formal diagnosis is required in all cases — functional impact is what matters.

2

Apply to Access to Work

Apply online at gov.uk/access-to-work or call 0800 121 7479. Tell them you need assistive software for your neurodivergent profile and name DysPhilter™ as your preferred tool.

3

Workplace Needs Assessment

An AtW adviser will arrange a Workplace Needs Assessment. The assessor will look at your work environment and recommend support. You can request DysPhilter™ as part of your recommendations.

4

Get your recommendations

Your AtW report will list recommended software, equipment, and support. It will name DysPhilter™ and any other tools or coaching recommended for your specific needs.

5

Order through Neurobox (or your chosen supplier)

Send your AtW report to Neurobox. They can supply DysPhilter™ and any other recommendations from your report — one supplier, one invoice, one point of contact. They will also provide training on how to use DysPhilter™ for your specific workplace needs.

6

Reimbursement

Your employer purchases the license and claims reimbursement from DWP. Self-employed individuals can claim directly. If you order through Neurobox, you get one itemised invoice — which means faster reimbursement.

Self-employed? You are eligible for 100% Access to Work funding. You apply directly — no employer needed. Call 0800 121 7479 or apply at gov.uk/access-to-work.

The grant does not affect your other benefits. You do not have to pay it back. Access to Work is separate from necessary adjustments — your employer still has their own legal duty under the Equality Act 2010.

Questions
Short answers to the things people ask
Do I need a diagnosis to use DysPhilter?

No. You do not need a diagnosis, a label, or a referral. DysPhilter is for everyone.

Is it really free for under 18s?

Yes. Completely free, with no limits. We believe support should not depend on someone's age or income.

Can I get it through Access to Work?

Yes. If you are working or self-employed, Access to Work can fund it for you. The government pays. You do not. See the funding section above.

Does it replace my other tools?

No. DysPhilter works alongside Microsoft 365, Google, WhatsApp, and everything else you already use. It sits beside your tools, not on top of them.

I am self-employed. Can I claim this as a business expense?

Yes. The Personal Edition includes self-employed invoicing. You can generate invoices from inside the app for your tax records.

What if I cannot afford it?

No one is turned away. Student and NHS discounts are available, and if the price is still a barrier, contact us.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your data is yours. We do not sell it, share it, or use it for advertising. Everything is encrypted.
